Preppin' Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Preppin' is your personalized meal planning and preparation assistant designed to simplify your life and help you eat healthier. Our service offers personalized meal plans, a recipe library, and flexible subscriptions that fit your lifestyle and budget to make meal planning, shopping, and cooking easier and more enjoyable.

Click the "Explore Survey" option on our website. Select the survey that best fits your interests and dietary preferences. Fill out the survey to the best of your ability. After submitting your survey, click "Next" to access the payment page and choose your preferred subscription. Within 24 hours after processing your payment, you will receive a comprehensive nutrition analysis and a personalized meal plan for the week at the email address you provided in the survey.

We offer three subscription plans to fit your needs and budget.

  • Monthly Plan: $9.99 per month
  • 6-Month Plan: $5.99 per month
  • 1-Year Plan: $4.17 per month

Choose the plan that works best for you and enjoy personalized meal planning and nutrition analysis tailored to your preferences.

To cancel or change your subscription, please email preppinteam@gmail.com. Your request will be processed within 24 hours.

Refunds will be issued on a case-by-case basis. Please email your request and reasoning to preppinteam@gmail.com. Our team will review your request and let you know the outcome within 24 hours.

At Preppin', we take your privacy seriously. The answers you provide in our surveys are used solely to create your personalized nutritional analysis and meal plan. Your data is handled securely and confidentially, and we do not use your personal information beyond the necessary scope for providing our services.

Still have questions?

Email preppinteam@gmail.com and we will reach out to you regarding your inquiry within 24 hours.
